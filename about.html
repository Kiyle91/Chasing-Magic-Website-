About Chasing Magic Studios

Cozy fantasy, heartfelt storytelling, and tactical play.

Studio Story

Chasing Magic Studios is an indie team building warm fantasy games that blend strategy with character-driven stories.

Our goal is to make worlds that feel welcoming while still giving players meaningful tactical choices.

What Is Olivia's World: Crystal Keep?

Olivia's World: Crystal Keep is a cozy fantasy tower-defence adventure. Players protect crystal spires by choosing from unique Guardians, each with distinct abilities and personality.

The game is designed as the first chapter of a larger story, where magic, memory, and courage shape each battle.

Accessibility and Cozy Values

  • Readable interfaces and clear visual signals to support comfortable play sessions.
  • A calm fantasy tone with supportive storytelling and low-pressure exploration moments.
  • Guardian variety so players can choose styles that fit their preferences.
  • Design intent focused on inclusion, clarity, and emotional warmth.
