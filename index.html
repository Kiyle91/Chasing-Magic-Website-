Olivia’s World: Crystal Keep is a cosy fantasy tower-defence adventure and the opening chapter of a larger tale. Across a fragile world shaped by magic and memory, crystal spires stand as the last line of hope.

Step into the roles of unique Guardians, each with their own powers, personality, and perspective on the story unfolding around them. Defend the spires against rising enemy waves, adapting your strategy as the threat grows.

Explore gentle, story-driven moments that hint at a much larger saga yet to come.

Rely upon enchanted steeds and special abilities when courage alone is not enough.

This is a world where bravery is not about being fearless.

It is about standing firm, even when you doubt yourself.

And choosing to protect what matters.
